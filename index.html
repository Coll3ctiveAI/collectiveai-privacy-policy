Privacy Policy for Collective.AI

Effective Date: April 8, 2025

1. Data Collection and Usage

What data is collected:

We collect personal information such as email addresses (via Firebase Authentication), usage data including app activity, and technical details such as device model and operating system.

How data is collected:

Data is collected through user input, Firebase SDKs, and third-party APIs including ChatGPT (OpenAI), Claude (Anthropic), Gemini (Google), DeepSeek, and Mathpix.

Why data is collected:

Data is collected to authenticate users, improve app performance, support AI interactions, enable subscriptions, and provide personalized AI responses.

Data sharing:

Data may be shared with Firebase (Google), RevenueCat, and our AI providers (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, Mathpix) to deliver core functionality. No data is sold to third parties.

Data retention/deletion:

Account and usage data is stored in Firebase Firestore and may be retained until the user requests deletion via our support contact.

Data security:

We implement Firebase Security Rules and HTTPS encryption to protect data. Only authorized team members have access to stored data.

2. User Rights and Control

Consent:

Users consent to data collection by signing in and using the app. Consent can be revoked by discontinuing use or requesting account deletion.

Access and correction:

Users may contact us to access or correct their data stored in Firebase.

Opt-out:

Users may opt out of certain analytics tracking by disabling data sharing through device settings or by contacting support.

Data deletion:

To request deletion of your data, email us at support@collectiveai.app.

Contact information:

Email: support@collectiveai.app
