Support

If you're experiencing any issues with Collective.AI or have questions about subscriptions, billing, or technical problems, we’re here to help.

🔐 Login Help

You can sign in by tapping Continue (anonymous login) or Sign in with Apple. No password or email is required to begin using the app.

💳 Subscription Support

If you're having trouble with subscriptions or purchases:

  • Ensure you're using a TestFlight build (if in testing phase)
  • Make sure you are signed into your Apple ID and the app shows an active subscription
  • If you’ve made a purchase and it didn’t go through, tap “Restore Purchases” in the app
